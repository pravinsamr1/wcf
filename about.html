Welcome to MoM & BabY Hospital

(Formerly known as WCF Hospital)

We are pleased to announce our transformation into MoM & BabY Hospital — a name that reflects our deeper commitment to specialized care for mothers, babies, and families. This marks our evolution into a more specialized, modern, and family-centered healthcare destination.

New Identity, Same Trust

The Oldest & Most Trusted Maternity Hospital in Chennai

MoM & BabY Hospital, T. Nagar is the oldest and most established branch of our network, serving the community with exceptional healthcare for over 25 years. With a legacy of excellence in women’s health, maternity care, paediatrics, and advanced surgeries, we continue to lead with a patient-first approach.

Led by highly experienced specialists and a compassionate management team, we ensure every patient receives personalized care. Our rebranding represents our core focus: Comprehensive Maternity Care, Advanced Neonatal services, and specialized support for women at every stage of life.

Mission

To provide holistic, comprehensive healthcare under one roof at an affordable cost, specialized for mothers and children.

Vision

Raising the standards of healthcare to make it Affordable, Equitable, and family-centered for every stage of life.

Why the Change?

Our new name, Mom and Baby Hospital, is more than just a title—it is a reflection of our evolution into a specialized, modern, and family-centered healthcare destination.
